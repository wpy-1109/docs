## Traffic types

| Traffic Type | Cost |
|-------------|------|
| **Inbound (upload)** | Free |
| **Internal (same-region ECS)** | Free (via internal endpoint) |
| **Outbound internet** | Per-GB fee |
| **Cross-region replication** | Per-GB fee |
| **Transfer acceleration** | Per-GB fee (in addition to outbound) |
| **OSS to CDN (back-to-origin)** | Per-GB fee (cheaper than direct outbound) |

## Cost-Saving tips

1. **Use internal endpoints** from same-region ECS instances
2. **Use CDN** for frequently downloaded content (CDN back-to-origin traffic is cheaper)
3. **Enable pay-by-requester** for shared data scenarios
4. **Use transfer acceleration** only when needed (adds cost but improves speed)
5. **Compress data** before upload to reduce storage and transfer costs

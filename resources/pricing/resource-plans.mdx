## Storage capacity units (SCUs)

Prepaid storage packages that offset pay-as-you-go storage fees:

| Term | Discount |
|------|----------|
| 1 month | ~15% off |
| 6 months | ~30% off |
| 1 year | ~50% off |

### How sCUs work

1. Purchase an SCU for a specific capacity (e.g., 100 GB, 1 TB, 10 TB)
2. SCU automatically offsets storage fees each billing cycle
3. Usage beyond the SCU capacity is billed at pay-as-you-go rates
4. SCUs apply to Standard, IA, and Archive storage classes

### Purchasing

1. Go to the OSS console > **Resource Plans**
2. Select the storage capacity and term
3. Complete the purchase
4. The SCU takes effect immediately

## Traffic plans

Prepaid outbound traffic packages available for high-traffic scenarios.

## Choosing the right plan

| Scenario | Recommendation |
|----------|---------------|
| Variable workload, testing | Pay-as-you-go |
| Predictable storage, 6+ months | SCU (6-month or 1-year) |
| High outbound traffic | Traffic plan |
| Short-term project | Pay-as-you-go or 1-month SCU |

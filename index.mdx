## Key capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Flexible storage classes" icon="layer-group">
    Standard, Infrequent Access, Archive, Cold Archive, and Deep Cold Archive tiers to optimize cost and performance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data protection" icon="shield-check">
    Server-side encryption, versioning, cross-region replication, and WORM retention policies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Access control" icon="lock">
    Fine-grained permissions with RAM policies, bucket policies, ACLs, STS temporary credentials, and presigned URLs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data processing" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Built-in image processing, video snapshots, content detection, and AI-powered data indexing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Acceleration" icon="bolt">
    Transfer acceleration, CDN integration, and global acceleration for low-latency access worldwide.
  </Card>
  <Card title="S3 compatibility" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Compatible with Amazon S3 APIs, making migration and multi-cloud workflows straightforward.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The OSS sandbox limits bucket access when abnormal behavior is detected, such as traffic spikes from hotlinking or DDoS attacks. In sandbox mode, download speeds are throttled and access may be intermittently unavailable.

## Prevention

- Enable [hotlink protection](/guides/security/hotlink-protection) to prevent unauthorized embedding.
- Use [CDN acceleration](/guides/networking/cdn-acceleration) to offload traffic.
- Configure [DDoS protection](/guides/security/ddos-protection) for high-traffic buckets.
- Monitor access patterns using [access logging](/guides/logging-monitoring/access-logging).

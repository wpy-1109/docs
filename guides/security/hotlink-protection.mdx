Hotlink protection prevents unauthorized websites from embedding or linking directly to your OSS objects. OSS checks the `Referer` header and allows or denies the request based on your whitelist.

## Configure hotlink protection

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Console">
    Navigate to your bucket, choose **Permission Control > Hotlink Protection**, add allowed Referer domains, and save.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Python">
    ```python
    client.put_bucket_referer(oss.PutBucketRefererRequest(
        bucket="examplebucket",
        referer_configuration=oss.RefererConfiguration(
            allow_empty_referer=False,
            referer_list=oss.RefererList(
                referers=["https://example.com", "https://*.example.com"]
            ),
        )
    ))
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

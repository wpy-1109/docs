WORM retention policies prevent objects from being modified or deleted during a specified retention period (1 day to 70 years). This is essential for regulatory compliance.

## How it works

1. **Initiate** a WORM policy with a retention period.
2. The policy enters a **pending** state for 24 hours.
3. **Complete** (lock) the policy to make it permanent.
4. You can **extend** the retention period but cannot shorten it.

<Warning>
Once locked, a WORM policy cannot be removed or shortened. Objects are protected until the retention period expires.
</Warning>

## Using the console

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable WORM">
    Navigate to your bucket, choose **Data Protection > WORM**, and click **Create Policy**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set retention period">
    Configure the retention period and click **OK**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock the policy">
    Within 24 hours, click **Lock** to make the policy permanent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

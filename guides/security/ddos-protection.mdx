OSS integrates with Anti-DDoS to defend against volumetric and application-layer attacks.

## Protection levels

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Basic** | Free, automatic protection against common attacks. Always enabled. |
| **Advanced** | Paid protection with higher thresholds and traffic scrubbing. |

## Enable advanced protection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Anti-DDoS instance">
    Purchase an Anti-DDoS Pro or Premium instance from the Alibaba Cloud console.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bind your bucket">
    In the OSS console, navigate to **Bucket Settings > DDoS Protection** and bind the bucket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

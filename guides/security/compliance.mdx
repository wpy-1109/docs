Alibaba Cloud OSS meets international and regional compliance certifications including ISO 27001, SOC 1/2/3, CSA STAR, PCI DSS, GDPR, and HIPAA.

## Compliance features in OSS

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| [WORM retention](/guides/security/worm-retention) | Immutable storage for regulatory requirements |
| [Server-side encryption](/guides/security/server-side-encryption) | Data encryption at rest |
| [Access logging](/guides/logging-monitoring/access-logging) | Audit trail for all bucket access |
| [Versioning](/guides/data-management/versioning) | Protection against accidental deletion |

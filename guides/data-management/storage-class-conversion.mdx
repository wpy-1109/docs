You can convert objects between storage classes to optimize costs. Use lifecycle rules for automatic conversion or manually change the storage class of individual objects.

## Conversion paths

| From | To | Method |
|------|-----|--------|
| Standard | IA, Archive, Cold Archive, Deep Cold Archive | Lifecycle rule or manual |
| IA | Archive, Cold Archive, Deep Cold Archive | Lifecycle rule or manual |
| Archive | Cold Archive, Deep Cold Archive | Lifecycle rule |

<Warning>
Objects in IA have a minimum storage duration of 30 days. Deleting or converting before 30 days incurs early deletion fees.
</Warning>

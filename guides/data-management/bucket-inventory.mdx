Bucket inventory generates regular reports listing all objects in a bucket, including metadata such as size, storage class, encryption status, and last modified date. Use inventory reports for auditing, compliance, and storage optimization.

## Report frequency

| Frequency | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Daily** | A report is generated every day. |
| **Weekly** | A report is generated every week. |

## Using the console

Navigate to your bucket, choose **Data Management > Bucket Inventory**, and click **Create Inventory**.

Global Accelerator (GA) uses Alibaba Cloud's BGP bandwidth and global transmission network to accelerate access from clients to OSS buckets across regions. Clients connect to the nearest GA access point, and traffic travels over the internal backbone network to the bucket's region.

## When to use

- Cross-border access with high latency, jitter, or packet loss.
- Dedicated acceleration with guaranteed bandwidth and SLA.
- Scenarios where transfer acceleration alone does not meet performance requirements.

<Note>
For most cross-region acceleration needs, [transfer acceleration](/guides/networking/transfer-acceleration) is simpler to configure. Use Global Accelerator when you need dedicated bandwidth, advanced traffic management, or integration with other Alibaba Cloud network services.
</Note>

## Prerequisites

- An OSS bucket with stored resources.
- A Global Accelerator instance (standard pay-as-you-go).

## Configure global accelerator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a GA instance">
    Go to the [GA console](https://ga.console.alibabacloud.com/list) and create a standard pay-as-you-go instance. Configure the acceleration area to match your users' locations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a listener">
    Add a listener with protocol and port settings that match your access pattern (typically HTTPS on port 443).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the endpoint group">
    Create an endpoint group in the region of your OSS bucket. Set the endpoint type to **Custom IP or Domain** and enter your OSS bucket's public endpoint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify acceleration">
    Access your OSS resources through the GA-assigned CNAME or IP address and compare latency with direct public endpoint access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing

GA instances use pay-by-data-transfer metering. Data transfer over the GA network is managed by Cloud Data Transfer (CDT). You also pay standard OSS traffic fees for origin access.

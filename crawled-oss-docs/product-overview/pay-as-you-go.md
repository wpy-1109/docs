| Item | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Billing rule | You are charged based on the resource usage. Bills are generated and fees are deducted from the balance of your Alibaba Cloud account after a billing cycle ends. |
| Scenario | The pay-as-you-go billing method is suitable for the following scenarios:Resource usage frequently fluctuates. Your business has short-term requirements on resource usage. |
| Billable item | The following items support the pay-as-you-go billing method:Storage feesTraffic feesAPI operation calling feesData processing feesObject tagging feesTransfer acceleration feesTemporary storage feesOSS DDoS protection feesData indexing feesRTC traffic fees |
| Billing cycle | Bills are generated on an hourly basis and settled on a monthly basis.Note If the total value of your account balance and the vouchers in your Alibaba Cloud account is less than the amount that you need to pay, you are notified by text messages or emails. |
| Pricing | For more information about the pricing of the pay-as-you-go billing method, visit the OSS Pricing page. |

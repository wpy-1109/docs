Cross-region replication (CRR) automatically copies objects from a source bucket to a destination bucket in a different region. This provides disaster recovery, compliance with data residency requirements, and reduced latency for geographically distributed users.

## Prerequisites

- Both source and destination buckets must have versioning enabled.
- The destination bucket must be in a different region.

## Using the console

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable versioning">
    Enable versioning on both the source and destination buckets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure replication">
    Navigate to the source bucket, choose **Data Management > Cross-Region Replication**, and click **Enable**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select destination">
    Choose the destination region and bucket, configure replication scope, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

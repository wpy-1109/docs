Archive direct reading allows you to access Archive and Cold Archive objects without manually initiating a restore. OSS automatically restores the object on demand when accessed.

## Prerequisites

- SDK installed and configured ([quickstart](/quickstart))

## Enable archive direct reading

```python
client.put_bucket_archive_direct_read(oss.PutBucketArchiveDirectReadRequest(
    bucket="examplebucket",
    archive_direct_read_configuration=oss.ArchiveDirectReadConfiguration(
        enabled=True
    )
))
```

<Note>
Archive direct reading incurs additional retrieval fees. Use standard restore for cost-sensitive workloads.
</Note>
